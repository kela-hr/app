Privacy Policy for Kela HR

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Kela HR ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Kela HR Chrome Extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.

Information We Collect

The extension uses the current tab's URL (LinkedIn profile page) to identify the profile ID.

Users may manually enter contact details such as email addresses and phone numbers associated with a LinkedIn profile.

How We Use Information

The information is used to help users organize and manage LinkedIn contacts within the extension.

Data Storage

Data entered by users is transmitted to and stored on our servers via API calls.

The stored data is associated with LinkedIn profile identifiers and is accessible to other users of the extension for the same profile.

Data Sharing

Data entered into the extension may be visible to and editable by other users of the extension when viewing the same LinkedIn profile.

We do not sell user data to third parties.

User Responsibility

Users should not enter sensitive personal information into the extension. By entering data, users acknowledge that the information may be accessible to other users of the extension.

Permissions Used

The extension uses the activeTab permission to access the current tab URL, which is required to identify LinkedIn profile pages.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data stored on our servers; however, we cannot guarantee complete security.

Target Audience

This extension is intended for LinkedIn users who want to manage and organize contacts. It is not directed toward children under the age of 13.
